Five words that define how Megan sounds

Earthy 27 acres in Floyd, VA — creek bathing, spring water, real land. Copy feels like soil under its fingernails.
Feral Raw land, camper, vision. Women come to shed the performance, not polish it.
Cosmic Stars, charts, transits — always embodied, felt in the body first.
Pleasure-positive Pole dancing at Beltane. Flower crowns. Sacred and sensual are the same thing here.
Direct No Instagram wellness. No spiritual bypassing. Every word earns itself or gets cut.

How she builds a sentence

Short declarative → opens into body

"No Instagram wellness. No spiritual bypassing. Just earth, fire, stars, and the freedom to be exactly who you are."

"Remember" as verb and homecoming

"Where women gather to shed the performance and remember their truth."

Cosmic + body, always together

"Feel it in your bones — not just understand in your head. That's what embodied astrology means."

Place as a character

"Nestled on 27 acres of quiet meadowland in Floyd, VA — return to what's wild and natural."

The language map

✓ Say this

  • "Remember" — the brand's core motion; women come to reclaim what they forgot
  • "Wild women / feral women" — her exact audience language; own it
  • "The Meadow" — always a proper noun; the land is a named character
  • "Embodied" — felt in the body, not just understood in the head
  • "Shed the performance" — the transformation mechanism
  • Both/and contrast pairs — "a little crunchy, a lot glam"
  • Em dashes — she thinks in dashes; the copy should too

× Avoid this

  • "Manifest / manifestation" — too Instagram, no texture for her brand
  • "Healing journey" — say what the journey actually is
  • "Your highest / best self" — generic, not her voice
  • "Transform your life" — too vague, no felt sense
  • "Ancient wisdom" — say which tradition, which practice specifically
  • "It's time to..." — every wellness brand on Instagram uses this
  • Distance-creating polish — this brand lives close; copy should too
